<p><strong>The Respiratory Protection Equipment (RPE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Negative Pressure APR</li><li>Positive Pressure PAPR</li><li>Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Respiratory Protection Equipment (RPE) includes Negative Pressure Air-Purifying Respirator (APR), Positive Pressure Powered Air-Purifying Respirator (PAPR), and Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA). Negative Pressure APR relies on the wearer's inhalation to create a vacuum inside the mask, while Positive Pressure PAPR uses a powered fan to push filtered air into the mask. SCBA provides a self-contained air supply for more hazardous environments. These different types of RPE cater to various levels of respiratory protection needs in different work settings.</p></p>
