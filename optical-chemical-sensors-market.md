<p><p>Optical chemical sensors are categorized into different types based on their technology and method of detection. Infrared sensors use infrared light to detect chemical substances. Fibre optic sensors utilize light transmission through optical fibers for sensing purposes. Photoionization sensors work by measuring the ionization potential of molecules. Other types of optical chemical sensors include fluorescence sensors, Raman sensors, and colorimetric sensors. Each type has its own advantages and applications in various industries such as environmental monitoring, healthcare, and industrial processes.</p></p>
