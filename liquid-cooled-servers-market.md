<p><p>Liquid cooled servers are data center servers that use liquid cooling technology to dissipate heat generated by the components. This technology involves circulating a coolant liquid, such as water or mineral oil, through the server components to effectively reduce their operating temperatures. Liquid cooled servers are becoming increasingly popular in data centers due to their ability to provide more efficient cooling compared to traditional air-cooled servers.</p><p>The liquid cooled servers market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.4% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for high-performance computing, as well as the rising adoption of artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies, are driving the growth of the market. Liquid cooled servers offer higher efficiency in cooling, which allows for better performance and longer lifespan of the server components. Additionally, liquid cooling helps in reducing the overall energy consumption of data centers, making them more sustainable and cost-effective.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the liquid cooled servers market include the development of advanced cooling technologies, such as direct-to-chip liquid cooling and immersion cooling. These technologies offer even more efficient cooling solutions for high-density computing applications. Furthermore, the increasing focus on green data centers and the growing trend of edge computing are also expected to drive the demand for liquid cooled servers in the coming years.</p></p>
