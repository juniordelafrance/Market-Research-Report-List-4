<p><strong>The Electric Power Assisted Steering Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPAS</li><li>P-EPAS</li><li>R-EPAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Assisted Steering Systems (EPAS) can be classified into three main types: Column Electric Power Assisted Steering (C-EPAS), Pinion Electric Power Assisted Steering (P-EPAS), and Rack Electric Power Assisted Steering (R-EPAS). </p><p>C-EPAS systems have the electric motor located on the steering column, P-EPAS have the motor attached to the steering pinion, and R-EPAS have the motor integrated with the steering rack. Each type offers different advantages in terms of steering feel, responsiveness, and energy efficiency, catering to various vehicle designs and driving preferences.</p></p>
