<p><p>Disk ripper market types include single action, offset type, and double action. Single action disk rippers consist of a single row of disks that work directly behind the tractor. Offset type disk rippers have a row of disks placed slightly off-center to provide greater clearance for residue. Double action disk rippers have two rows of disks, allowing for more aggressive soil tillage. Each type caters to different farming needs and soil conditions, offering versatility and customization for farmers.</p></p>
