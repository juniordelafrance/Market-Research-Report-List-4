<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Uncooled Infrared Microbolometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vox Microbolometer</li><li>α-Si Microbolometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Uncooled infrared microbolometer market types include Vox microbolometers and α-Si microbolometers. Vox microbolometers use a vanadium oxide (VOx) material as the sensing element, providing high sensitivity and thermal resolution. On the other hand, α-Si microbolometers use amorphous silicon as the sensing material, offering a cost-effective solution with decent performance. Both types are popular choices for uncooled infrared imaging applications due to their compact size, low power consumption, and compatibility with various thermal imaging systems.</p></p>
