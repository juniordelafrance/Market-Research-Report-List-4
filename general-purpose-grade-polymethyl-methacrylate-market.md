Extruded Sheet is a continuous process where PMMA is forced through a die to form a sheet. Cast Acrylic Sheet is made by pouring PMMA into molds and allowing it to solidify. Pellets are small, uniform pieces of PMMA used for molding and extrusion processes. Beads are small spherical particles of PMMA used for various applications like injection molding and coatings.</p></p>
