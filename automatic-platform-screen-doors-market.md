<p><strong>The Automatic Platform Screen Doors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Closed Type</li><li>Semi-Closed Type</li><li>Half Height Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic Platform Screen Doors (APSD) are classified into three types based on their level of closure: Full-Closed Type, Semi-Closed Type, and Half Height Type. Full-Closed Type APSDs completely seal off the platform from the tracks, providing maximum safety and security. Semi-Closed Type APSDs have a partial barrier between the platform and the tracks, offering a compromise between safety and accessibility. Half Height Type APSDs have barriers that only cover the lower portion of the platform, allowing for easy access while still providing some level of protection.</p></p>
