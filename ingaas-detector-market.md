<p><p>The InGaAs detector market is primarily segmented into two types: multi InGaAs photodiode arrays and single InGaAs photodiodes. Multi InGaAs photodiode arrays consist of multiple sensors arranged together, allowing for high-speed and high-sensitivity detection across a broad spectrum, making them ideal for applications like spectral analysis and imaging.
