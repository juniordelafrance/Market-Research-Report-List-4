<p><p>The Lithium-Metal Secondary Battery market includes two main types: Li/Intercalant Cathode and Li/Sulfur. Li/Intercalant Cathode batteries utilize lithium as the anode and an intercalation compound as the cathode, offering high energy density and longer lifespan. Both types show promise for advancing battery technology and are being researched and developed for various applications.</p></p>
