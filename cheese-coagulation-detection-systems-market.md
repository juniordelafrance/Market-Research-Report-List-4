<p><p>Contact cheese coagulation detection systems involve physical contact between the sensor and the cheese sample during the measurement process. This type of system is typically used in laboratory settings where precise measurements are required. On the other hand, non-contact cheese coagulation detection systems use methods such as spectroscopy or imaging to analyze the cheese sample without touching it. These systems are often used in industrial settings where quick, non-invasive measurements are needed for large-scale production processes.</p></p>
