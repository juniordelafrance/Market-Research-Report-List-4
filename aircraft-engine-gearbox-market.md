<p><strong>The Aircraft Engine Gearbox Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reduction Gearbox (RGB)</li><li>Accessory Gearbox (AGB)</li><li>Actuation Gearbox</li><li>Tail Rotor Gearbox</li><li>APU-Gearbox</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reduction Gearbox (RGB) is used to reduce the high-speed output of the engine to a lower speed suitable for the propeller or rotor. Accessory Gearbox (AGB) drives various accessories such as the fuel pump, generators, and hydraulic pumps. Actuation Gearbox is responsible for controlling power distribution to different aircraft components. Tail Rotor Gearbox is used to drive the tail rotor in helicopters. APU-Gearbox powers the auxiliary power unit. Other types of gearboxes are used for specific functions such as ice protection or environmental control systems.</p></p>
