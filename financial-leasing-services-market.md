<p><p>Financial leasing services market includes three main types: </p><p>1. Capital Lease: Also known as a finance lease, where the lessee assumes substantial risks and rewards of ownership of the asset. The lessee basically owns the asset during the lease term.</p><p>2. Operating Lease: In this type of lease, the lessor retains ownership of the asset and the lessee only uses it for a specific period without owning it. It is commonly used for short-term assets.</p><p>3. Others: This category includes specialized leases tailored to specific industries or assets, such as sale and leaseback or leveraged lease agreements.</p></p>
