<p><strong>The Jet Bridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Apron drive jet bridge</li><li>Nose-loader jet bridge</li><li>Dual jet bridge</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jet bridges are available in various types to cater to different needs at airports. Apron drive jet bridges are stationary and attach directly to the terminal building, providing a stable platform for passengers to board the aircraft. Nose-loader jet bridges are designed for aircraft with front doors lower to the ground, allowing for easy access for passengers. Dual jet bridges consist of two connected walkways, enabling simultaneous boarding and deplaning, making the process more efficient and reducing passenger wait times. These different types cater to the specific requirements of various aircraft and airport setups.</p></p>
