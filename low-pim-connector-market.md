Wire to Wire connectors facilitate direct connections between two wires, ensuring minimal passive intermodulation. Wire to Board connectors link wires to a circuit board, optimizing signal integrity. Board to Board connectors connect two printed circuit boards, enhancing system compactness while reducing interference. Each type is crucial in telecommunications and RF applications, prioritizing performance and reliability in high-frequency environments.</p></p>
