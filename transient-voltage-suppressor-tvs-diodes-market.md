<p><p>Transient Voltage Suppressor (TVS) diodes can be classified into two main types: uni-polar TVS and bi-polar TVS. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed to protect against positive voltage spikes, while bi-polar TVS diodes can handle both positive and negative voltage spikes. Uni-polar TVS diodes are commonly used in applications where only one polarity of voltage needs to be suppressed, while bi-polar TVS diodes are used in applications where both positive and negative voltage spikes need to be controlled.</p></p>
