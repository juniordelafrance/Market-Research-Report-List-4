<p><p>Semiconductor wet etching systems are used for etching different types of materials in the semiconductor industry. Dielectric etching involves the removal of dielectric materials such as silicon dioxide or silicon nitride. Silicon etching is used to selectively remove silicon from substrates. Metal etching is used for etching metal layers in semiconductor devices. Other types of etching include compound semiconductor etching, MEMS (Microelectromechanical Systems) etching, and glass etching. Each type of etching process requires specific chemicals and equipment for precise material removal.</p></p>
