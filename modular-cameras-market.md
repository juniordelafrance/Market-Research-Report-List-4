<p><strong>The Modular Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD</li><li>CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modular cameras market is primarily categorized into two types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD cameras are known for their high image quality and low noise levels, making them ideal for professional applications. However, they tend to consume more power. Conversely, CMOS cameras offer lower power consumption, faster speeds, and integration capabilities, making them popular in consumer electronics and mobile devices. This divergence reflects the growing demand for diverse imaging solutions across various sectors.</p></p>
