<p><p>A respiratory heater is a medical device used to provide warmth and humidity to inspired gases during respiratory therapy. It is commonly used in hospitals and clinics to prevent hypothermia and other complications in patients receiving mechanical ventilation. The device heats and humidifies the air before it enters the patient's airway, helping to maintain the patient's body temperature and prevent damage to the respiratory tract.</p><p>The respiratory heater market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.40% during the forecast period. The market growth is driven by the increasing prevalence of respiratory diseases, such as chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD) and asthma, which require respiratory therapy. Additionally, the rising geriatric population and the increasing number of premature births are contributing to the market growth, as these populations are more prone to respiratory issues.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the respiratory heater market is the development of portable and battery-operated devices, which offer greater convenience and flexibility for healthcare providers. These devices are especially useful in homecare settings, where patients may require respiratory therapy outside of a traditional hospital environment. Manufacturers are also focusing on improving the efficiency and accuracy of their devices to enhance patient outcomes and reduce the risk of complications.</p></p>
