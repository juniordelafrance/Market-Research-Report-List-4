<p><strong>The Liquid Flow Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement Flow Meters</li><li>Turbine Flow Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Positive Displacement Flow Meters are mechanical flow meters that measure the volume of liquid passing through by trapping it in defined pockets and moving it along to measure the total volume. Turbine Flow Meters, on the other hand, use a rotating turbine to measure the flow rate of liquid passing through. Both types of flow meters are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and pharmaceuticals for accurate measurement of liquid flow rates.</p></p>
