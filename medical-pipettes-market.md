<p><p>Medical pipettes are essential tools used in laboratories for precise measurement and transfer of liquids. There are two main types of pipettes in the market: single channel and multichannel pipettes. Single channel pipettes allow for dispensing of one liquid at a time, making them suitable for applications requiring individual samples. On the other hand, multichannel pipettes can dispense multiple samples simultaneously, increasing efficiency and throughput in high-throughput screening and other applications that require handling of multiple samples at once.</p></p>
