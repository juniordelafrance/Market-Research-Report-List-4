<p><p>The Marine Grade Inertial Navigation System market can be classified into several types based on the type of gyroscope technology used. Mechanical Gyro, Ring Laser Gyro, Fiber Optics Gyro, and MEMS are the main types of inertial navigation systems used in marine applications. Mechanical Gyro relies on spinning masses to detect orientation changes, Ring Laser Gyro uses laser beams to measure angular velocity, Fiber Optics Gyro uses light waves confined in optical fibers, and MEMS relies on microscopic sensors to calculate movement. Other types of systems may also be used in specific applications.</p></p>
