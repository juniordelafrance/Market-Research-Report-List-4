This water can contain various organisms, including bacteria, microbes, small invertebrates, and larvae of marine species. <p><ul><li>Chemical Method</li><li>Physical Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ballast Water Market includes two main types of treatment methods: Chemical and Physical. </p><p>Chemical methods involve the use of disinfectants or biocides to kill or inhibit the growth of harmful organisms in ballast water. This method is effective in treating large volumes of water quickly.</p><p>Physical methods, on the other hand, involve processes such as filtration, UV radiation, and heat treatment to remove or destroy organisms in ballast water. This method is considered more environmentally friendly as it does not involve the use of chemicals. Both methods play a vital role in preventing the spread of invasive species through ballast water discharge.</p></p>
