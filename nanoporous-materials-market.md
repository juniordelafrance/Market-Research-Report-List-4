<p><p>Nanoporous materials are classified into various types based on their composition and structure. Zeolites are crystalline aluminosilicates with uniform pore sizes. Clays are layered silicates with high surface area. Activated carbon is highly porous carbon material with adsorption properties. Silica gel is a porous form of silicon dioxide. Activated alumina is a porous form of aluminum oxide. Other nanoporous materials include metal-organic frameworks and porous polymers. Each type of nanoporous material offers unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
