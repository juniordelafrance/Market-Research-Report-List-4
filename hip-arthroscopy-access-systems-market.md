<p><p>Hip arthroscopy access systems are tools used for minimally invasive hip surgeries. There are two main types of access systems: Pincer type and Cam type. Pincer type access systems utilize a grasping mechanism to securely hold tissue and provide better access to the hip joint. Cam type access systems have a rotating mechanism that allows for precise movements and access to hard-to-reach areas in the hip joint. Both types of access systems are essential for successful hip arthroscopy procedures.</p></p>
