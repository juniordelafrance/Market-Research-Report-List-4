<p><p>Permanent Modular Construction involves building multi-family housing units or developments using off-site construction methods and then permanently installing them on-site. This type of modular construction is meant to be a long-term solution for residential housing needs.</p><p>Relocatable Modular Construction, on the other hand, involves building multi-family housing units or developments using off-site construction methods, but with the flexibility to be easily relocated to a different site in the future. This type of modular construction is often used for temporary or mobile housing solutions.</p></p>
