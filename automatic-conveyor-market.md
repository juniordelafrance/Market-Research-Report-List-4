<p><p>Automatic conveyor market consists of various types such as belt conveyors, vibrating conveyors, pneumatic conveyors, horizontal motion conveyors, screw conveyors, and others. Belt conveyors use a belt to transport items, vibrating conveyors use vibrations to move items, pneumatic conveyors use air pressure to transport materials, horizontal motion conveyors move items with a back-and-forth motion, and screw conveyors use rotating helical screws to move materials. Other types include bucket, chain, and roller conveyors, each with their own unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
