<p><p>Herpes testing kits are available in various types, including antibody/antigen based kits, viral culture test kits, and nucleic acid amplification-based kits. Antibody/antigen based kits detect antibodies or antigens produced in response to the herpes virus. Viral culture test kits involve growing the virus in a laboratory setting. Nucleic acid amplification-based kits detect the genetic material of the virus. Each type of testing kit has its own advantages and limitations, providing healthcare providers with different options for diagnosing herpes infections.</p></p>
