<p><p>Fixed-focus lenses are categorized into three main market types based on their iris control - Fixed Iris, Manual Iris, and Auto Iris. Fixed Iris lenses have a fixed aperture, providing a constant amount of light to the sensor. Manual Iris lenses allow the user to manually adjust the aperture to control the amount of light passing through. Auto Iris lenses automatically adjust the aperture based on the available light, ensuring consistent exposure in changing lighting conditions. Each market type offers unique benefits for different applications.</p></p>
