<p><p>Iodide Ion Selective Electrodes are available in three main types: Crystal Membrane, Liquid Membrane, and PVC Membrane. </p><p>Crystal Membrane electrodes utilize a crystalline material that selectively interacts with iodide ions. Liquid Membrane electrodes have a liquid membrane that allows selective permeation of iodide ions. PVC Membrane electrodes consist of a polyvinyl chloride membrane that selectively interacts with iodide ions.</p><p>Each type of electrode offers unique advantages and is suited for different applications in the measurement of iodide ions.</p></p>
