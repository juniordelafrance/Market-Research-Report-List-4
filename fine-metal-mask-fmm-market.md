<p><strong>The Fine Metal Mask (FMM) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Etching</li><li>Electroforming</li><li>Multi Material Composite Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fine Metal Mask (FMM) market consists of three main types: etching, electroforming, and multi-material composite method. Etching involves using chemicals to selectively remove metal layers to create the desired pattern. Electroforming utilizes electroplating techniques to deposit metal on a custom mold. The multi-material composite method combines different materials to create intricate designs. Each method offers unique advantages in terms of precision, cost-effectiveness, and versatility, catering to various industry needs in electronics, semiconductors, and other advanced technologies.</p></p>
