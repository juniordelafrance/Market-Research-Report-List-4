<p><p>Nut harvesters are machines used to efficiently collect nuts from orchards or groves. There are three main types of nut harvesters: self-propelled nut harvesters, trailed nut harvesters, and mounted nut harvesters. </p><p>Self-propelled nut harvesters are standalone machines capable of moving on their own, providing flexibility and independence during harvesting. Trailed nut harvesters are towed behind a tractor or other vehicle, offering more control and precision in narrow orchards. Mounted nut harvesters are attached to tractors or other vehicles, increasing efficiency and reducing manual labor during the harvesting process.</p></p>
