<p><p>Agricultural irrigation pumps come in various types to suit different needs. Submersible pumps are placed underwater to pump water up to the surface. Self-priming pumps can create a vacuum to draw water into the pump without needing additional priming. Vortex pumps are designed to handle mud, sludge, and other debris without clogging. Other types of pumps in the market include centrifugal pumps, diaphragm pumps, and piston pumps, each offering unique features and capabilities to cater to specific irrigation requirements.</p></p>
