<p><p>Portable Ultra Violet (UV) Lights are available in three main types: UVA Lights, UVB Lights, and UVC Lights. UVA Lights have the longest wavelengths and are commonly used for tanning beds, photocatalytic air purifiers, and insect traps. UVB Lights have medium wavelengths and are often used for medical treatments like psoriasis and eczema. UVC Lights have the shortest wavelengths and are widely used for disinfection purposes in water treatment, air purifiers, and surface sterilization. Each type of UV light has specific applications and benefits in the market.</p></p>
