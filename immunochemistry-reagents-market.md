<p><ul><li>Primary Antibody</li><li>Secondary Antibody</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Immunochemistry reagents market consists of primary antibodies and secondary antibodies. Primary antibodies are directly bind to the target antigen, while secondary antibodies bind to the primary antibodies, amplifying the signal and enabling detection. Primary antibodies are used for specific antigen detection, while secondary antibodies are used for signal amplification in techniques like Western blotting and immunohistochemistry. Both types of antibodies play a crucial role in immunochemistry assays for research and diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
