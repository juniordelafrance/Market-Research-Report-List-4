<p><p>Flanged Progressing Cavity Pumps are a type of positive displacement pump that is commonly used for moving viscous fluids like sludge, wastewater, and oil. The pump consists of a helical rotor that rotates inside a double helix stator, creating cavities that draw in and push out the fluid. The flanged design allows for easy installation and maintenance of the pump, making it popular in industries such as wastewater treatment, oil & gas, and food processing.</p><p>The Flanged Progressing Cavity Pumps Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13% during the forecast period.
