<p><ul><li>Absorbed Glass Mat Battery</li><li>Gel Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>VRLA (Valve-Regulated Lead-Acid) batteries come in two main types: Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM) and Gel batteries. AGM batteries use fiberglass mats to absorb battery acid, offering high vibration resistance and spill-proof design. Gel batteries, on the other hand, use a silica gel as electrolyte, providing longer lifespan and better deep cycling capability. Both types are maintenance-free, sealed lead-acid batteries widely used in various applications such as UPS systems, electric vehicles, and solar energy storage.</p></p>
