<p><p>Vibration forming compactors can be divided into two main types: round indenter and rectangular indenter. Round indenter compactors use a circular shaping tool to compact materials evenly, while rectangular indenter compactors use a rectangular shaping tool for more precise shaping and molding. Both types of compactors use vibrations to settle and compress materials, creating dense and uniform structures. The choice between round and rectangular indenters depends on the specific requirements of the project and the desired outcome of the compacting process.</p></p>
