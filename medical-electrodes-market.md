<p><p>Medical electrodes market is divided into two main types: Disposable electrodes and Reusable electrodes. Disposable electrodes are single-use electrodes that are discarded after one patient use, making them hygienic and convenient for one-time procedures. On the other hand, Reusable electrodes are designed to be cleaned and disinfected for multiple uses, offering cost savings and environmental benefits. Both types of electrodes play a crucial role in various medical procedures such as electrocardiography, EEG, EMG, and ECG.</p></p>
