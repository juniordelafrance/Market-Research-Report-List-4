Horizontal balers are typically used for high volumes of waste paper, with the loading and compressing process done horizontally. Vertical balers, on the other hand, are more compact and suitable for smaller volumes of waste paper, with the compression process done vertically. Both types of balers serve the same purpose of efficiently managing waste paper materials.</p></p>
