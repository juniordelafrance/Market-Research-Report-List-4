<p><strong>The Base Station Optical Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Receiver Module</li><li>Optical Transmitter Module</li><li>Optical Transceiver Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Base Station Optical Module Market is classified into three types - Optical Receiver Module, Optical Transmitter Module, and Optical Transceiver Module. Optical Receiver Modules are used to receive signals and convert them into electrical signals. Optical Transmitter Modules are used to convert electrical signals into optical signals for transmission. Optical Transceiver Modules combine the functions of both receiver and transmitter modules in a single device, enabling bi-directional communication on a single fiber. These modules play a crucial role in enabling high-speed data transmission in base stations.</p></p>
