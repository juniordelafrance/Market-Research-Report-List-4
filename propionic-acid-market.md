<p><p>The propionic acid market is characterized by three primary production processes: the Oxo process, the Reppe process, and the By-product process. The Oxo process synthesizes propionic acid from propanol and carbon monoxide using a catalyst, offering high purity and yield. The Reppe process derives it from acetylene and carbon monoxide, creating versatile compounds. The By-product process recovers propionic acid as a secondary product in the production of other chemicals, providing cost-effective operations while contributing to the overall supply.</p></p>
