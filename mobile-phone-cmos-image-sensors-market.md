<p><strong>The Mobile Phone CMOS Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stacked CMOS</li><li>Back-illuminated CMOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stacked CMOS image sensors are designed with multiple layers of sensor pixels stacked on top of each other, allowing for improved image quality and faster processing speeds. This technology is widely used in high-end smartphones and digital cameras. On the other hand, back-illuminated CMOS image sensors feature a design where the light-sensitive elements are placed on the backside of the sensor, allowing for better light sensitivity and noise reduction, making them ideal for low-light photography applications. Both types of sensors are widely used in the mobile phone market to improve image quality and performance.</p></p>
