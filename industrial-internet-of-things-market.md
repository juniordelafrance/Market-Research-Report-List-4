Wired Technology involves connecting devices using physical cables for reliable and secure communication. Wireless Technology utilizes radio waves to transmit data between devices, providing flexibility and mobility. Field Technology involves sensors and actuators that collect data from the physical environment and control industrial processes. Each type offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications in the Industrial Internet-of-Things ecosystem.</p></p>
