<p><p>Hot air tunnel market can be classified into single air duct and multiple air duct types based on the number of ducts used in the tunnel. In single air duct systems, there is only one duct that carries the hot air throughout the tunnel. On the other hand, multiple air duct systems use more than one duct to distribute hot air evenly. Single air duct systems are simpler and more cost-effective, while multiple air duct systems offer better temperature control and uniform heating distribution.</p></p>
