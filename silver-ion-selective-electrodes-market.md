<p><p>The Silver Ion Selective Electrodes market comprises two primary types: Crystal Membrane and PVC Membrane. Crystal Membrane electrodes utilize a crystalline structure to selectively respond to silver ions, offering high stability and precision in measurements. In contrast, PVC Membrane electrodes are composed of polyvinyl chloride, providing flexibility and suitability for varied environmental conditions. Both types are essential in applications like water quality monitoring and chemical analysis, allowing accurate detection of silver ion concentrations in different samples.</p></p>
