<p><p>The Spiral Tubeformer Market consists of two main types: Tube Mold Type and Steel-Band Type. Tube Mold Type machines use molds to shape metal strips into spiral tubes of various diameters and lengths, offering precise and customizable results. On the other hand, Steel-Band Type machines rely on a continuous steel band that is formed into spirals, providing efficient production of spiral tubes. Both types cater to different needs in the HVAC and ventilation industry, offering diverse options for manufacturers.</p></p>
