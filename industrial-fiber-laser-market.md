<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser is a type of industrial fiber laser that emits a continuous beam of high-power laser light. It is suitable for applications requiring steady laser output, such as welding and cutting. On the other hand, Pulsed Fiber Laser market offers lasers that emit pulses of laser light at high peak power levels, making them ideal for applications like marking, engraving, and micro-machining. Pulsed lasers provide precise control over energy output and are often used for delicate materials processing.</p></p>
