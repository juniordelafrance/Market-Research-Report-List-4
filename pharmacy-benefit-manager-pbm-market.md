<p><p>A Pharmacy Benefit Manager (PBM) is a third-party administrator that manages prescription drug programs for health insurers, self-insured employers, and government agencies. PBMs negotiate drug prices with manufacturers, develop formularies, process pharmacy claims, and administer drug utilization review programs to ensure the appropriate use of medications. They play a crucial role in controlling healthcare costs and improving patient outcomes by promoting the use of generic drugs and managing specialty medications.</p><p>The Pharmacy Benefit Manager (PBM) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.5% during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the rising demand for cost-effective prescription drug management solutions, increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, and the growing adoption of specialty drugs. PBMs are also expanding their services to include medication therapy management, telehealth services, and data analytics to improve patient care and drive better outcomes.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing a trend towards consolidation as PBMs seek to enhance their bargaining power with drug manufacturers and pharmacies.
