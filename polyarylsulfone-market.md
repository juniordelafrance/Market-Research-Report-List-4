<p><p>The Polyarylsulfone market includes several types such as PPSU, PSU, PEI, and PESU. PPSU (Polyphenylsulfone) is known for its high heat resistance and chemical resistance, making it ideal for medical equipment and plumbing components. PSU (Polysulfone) offers excellent mechanical properties and transparency, commonly used in electronics and automotive parts. PEI (Polyetherimide) is a high-performance material with excellent thermal stability, often used in aerospace and automotive industries. PESU (Polyethersulfone) provides outstanding toughness and dimensional stability, suitable for food service and water treatment applications.</p></p>
