<p><p>Pump feeders are used in bulk material handling to accurately dispense materials into a process. There are two main types: volumetric pump feeders and gravitational pump feeders. Volumetric pump feeders use a pump to control the flow of material, ensuring precise measurements. Gravitational pump feeders rely on gravity to move the material through the system. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various industries such as mining, agriculture, and food processing.</p></p>
