<p><p>M2M modems are categorized into different market types based on their technology and functionality. ADSL modems utilize asymmetric digital subscriber line technology to provide internet connectivity over traditional phone lines. VDSL modems use very high-speed digital subscriber line technology for faster data transmission over copper lines. Other types of M2M modems include cellular modems, satellite modems, and Ethernet modems, offering various options for machine-to-machine communication in different environments and applications. Each type of modem has specific features and advantages depending on the needs of the user.</p></p>
