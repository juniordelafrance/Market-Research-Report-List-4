<p><p>Powder core reactor market is divided into Non-Shielded Powder Core Reactor and Shielded Powder Core Reactor types. Non-Shielded Powder Core Reactors are used in applications where electromagnetic interference (EMI) is not a concern, such as inductor applications. On the other hand, Shielded Powder Core Reactors are designed to reduce EMI and noise in sensitive electronic devices, making them suitable for use in telecommunications equipment, medical devices, and other applications where EMI shielding is critical.</p></p>
