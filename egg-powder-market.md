<p><p>The egg powder market comprises various types, each serving unique culinary and nutritional purposes. Whole egg powder is made from dehydrated whole eggs, suitable for baking and cooking. Egg yolk powder contains only the yolk, rich in fats and proteins, ideal for sauces and custards. Egg albumen powder consists solely of the egg white, used for its high protein content in various food applications. Egg powder mix combines these elements for convenience, offering versatility in food preparation and enhancing product stability.</p></p>
