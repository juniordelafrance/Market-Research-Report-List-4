<p><p>The Ultrasonic Tooth Cleaning Machine market consists of two main types: Magnetostrictive Ultrasonic Dental Cleaner Machine and Piezoelectric Ultrasonic Dental Cleaner Machine. Magnetostrictive machines use a magnetic field to create ultrasonic vibrations for cleaning teeth, while Piezoelectric machines use a crystal that generates ultrasonic vibrations when an electric current is applied. Both types of machines are effective at removing plaque, tartar, and stains from teeth, providing a thorough and efficient cleaning for patients.</p></p>
