<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The HgCdTe Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LN2 Cooled</li><li>Thermoelectrically (TE) Cooled</li><li>Stirling Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>HgCdTe Infrared Detectors are used in three main market types: LN2 cooled, thermoelectrically (TE) cooled, and Stirling cooled. LN2 cooled detectors utilize liquid nitrogen for cooling, offering high sensitivity but requiring frequent maintenance. TE cooled detectors use thermoelectric coolers to maintain temperature stability, providing a balance between sensitivity and cooling efficiency. Stirling cooled detectors use Stirling engines for cooling, offering the highest sensitivity and stability but also requiring higher power consumption. Each market type caters to different needs and requirements in the infrared detector industry.</p></p>
