<p><strong>The Angiography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Ray Angiography</li><li>CT Angiography</li><li>MR Angiography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Angiography Equipment Market includes three main types: X-ray angiography, CT angiography, and MR angiography. X-ray angiography uses an injected contrast agent and X-rays to create detailed images of blood vessels. CT angiography utilizes a CT scanner to generate detailed cross-sectional images of blood vessels. MR angiography uses magnetic resonance imaging to produce detailed images of blood vessels without the need for ionizing radiation. Each type of angiography equipment serves specific purposes in diagnosing and treating various cardiovascular conditions.</p></p>
