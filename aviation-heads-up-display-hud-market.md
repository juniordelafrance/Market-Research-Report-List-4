<p><p>The aviation heads-up display (HUD) market encompasses two primary types: conventional HUD and augmented reality (AR)-based HUD. Conventional HUDs project essential flight data onto a transparent panel, allowing pilots to maintain situational awareness while keeping their head up. Meanwhile, AR-based HUDs enhance this experience by overlaying real-time data, graphics, and navigation information directly onto the pilot's field of vision, offering a more immersive and intuitive interface. Both types aim to improve flight safety and operational efficiency in modern aviation.</p></p>
