<p><p>Vinyl sheet piling market consists of two main types - VZ Type and VU Type. VZ Type sheet piling is designed with a flat web for increased structural strength and stability, making it ideal for heavy-duty marine applications. On the other hand, VU Type sheet piling features a corrugated profile for enhanced flexibility and adaptability, making it suitable for projects requiring a more versatile solution. Both types offer durability, easy installation, and resistance to corrosion, making them popular choices in the construction industry.</p></p>
