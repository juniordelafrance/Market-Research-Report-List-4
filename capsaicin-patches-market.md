<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Capsaicin Patches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transdermal Absorption</li><li>Sonophoresis</li><li>Iontophoresis</li><li>Microneedle Transdermal Delivery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capsaicin patches are used for pain relief by delivering the active ingredient, capsaicin, through the skin. Transdermal absorption involves the medication passing through the skin barrier. Sonophoresis uses ultrasound waves to enhance the penetration of capsaicin. Iontophoresis involves the use of a small electric current to drive capsaicin through the skin. Microneedle transdermal delivery utilizes tiny needles to create microchannels in the skin, allowing for better absorption of capsaicin. These different methods offer various options for delivering capsaicin effectively for pain management.</p></p>
