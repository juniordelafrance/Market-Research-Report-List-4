<p><ul><li>With Methylated Ingredients</li><li>Without Methylated Ingredients</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pain relief sprays can be categorized based on the presence of methylated ingredients. Sprays with methylated ingredients typically contain menthol or camphor, which provide a cooling sensation that can help numb the area of pain. On the other hand, sprays without methylated ingredients rely on different active ingredients such as lidocaine or capsaicin to provide pain relief through mechanisms like blocking nerve signals or desensitizing pain receptors. Both types of sprays aim to reduce pain and discomfort, offering consumers a variety of options for managing their symptoms.</p></p>
