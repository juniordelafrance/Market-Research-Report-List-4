<p><p>Robotic pool cleaners come in two main types based on their drive mechanism: Crawler Drive and Wheel Drive. Crawler Drive cleaners use rubber or PVC tracks to navigate the pool, providing better traction on vertical surfaces and stairs. Wheel Drive cleaners, on the other hand, use rubber or PVC wheels to move around the pool, offering easier maneuverability and faster cleaning speeds. Both types of cleaners are popular choices for pool owners looking for efficient and convenient ways to keep their pools clean.</p></p>
