<p><p>The market for balloons used in treating Peripheral Arterial Disease is segmented into three main types: Ordinary Balloon, Drug-eluting Balloon, and Special Balloon. Ordinary Balloons are used for standard angioplasty procedures to open narrowed or blocked arteries. Drug-eluting Balloons are coated with medication to help prevent re-narrowing of the artery. Special Balloons include cutting and scoring balloons that are used for more complex cases where plaque is especially hard or resistant. Each type of balloon offers specific benefits and is used based on the patient's individual condition.</p></p>
