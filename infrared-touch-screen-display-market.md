<p><strong>The Infrared Touch Screen Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stand-Alone HMI</li><li>Embedded HMI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared touch screen display market can be categorized into two main types: Stand-Alone HMI and Embedded HMI. Stand-Alone HMI refers to independent systems that operate autonomously, often used in kiosks or public information displays. In contrast, Embedded HMI integrates touch screen functionality within a larger device, such as industrial equipment or consumer electronics, allowing for seamless user interaction. Both types leverage infrared technology to enhance responsiveness and durability, catering to various industries including retail, manufacturing, and healthcare.</p></p>
