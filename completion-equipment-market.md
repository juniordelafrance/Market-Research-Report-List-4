<p><ul><li>Casings</li><li>Cementing Materials & Equipment</li><li>Perforating Guns</li><li>Gravel Packing</li><li>Wellheads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Completion equipment market consists of various types such as casings, cementing materials & equipment, perforating guns, gravel packing, and wellheads. Casings provide structural support to the wellbore, while cementing materials & equipment are used for sealing the wellbore. Perforating guns create holes in the casing to allow oil or gas to flow into the well. Gravel packing is used to prevent sand production. Wellheads are the equipment used to control the flow of oil or gas from the well.</p></p>
