<p><p>Agricultural rollers are used in farming to compact soil, crush clods, and remove air pockets. There are two main types of agricultural rollers: hydraulic rollers and non-hydraulic rollers. Hydraulic rollers are powered by hydraulic systems, making them more efficient and easier to use. Non-hydraulic rollers, on the other hand, rely on manual or tractor power for operation. Both types of rollers are essential for preparing seedbeds and ensuring proper seed-to-soil contact for optimal plant growth.</p></p>
