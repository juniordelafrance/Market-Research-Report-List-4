<p><p>High Frequency Thin Film Inductors are divided into two main market types, SMD Type and Plug-in Type. SMD Type inductors are surface mount devices that can be directly mounted on printed circuit boards, making them ideal for compact electronic designs. Plug-in Type inductors, on the other hand, are designed to be inserted into sockets or connectors on a circuit board. Both types cater to different application needs in the high-frequency electronics market, providing flexibility and versatility for design engineers.</p></p>
