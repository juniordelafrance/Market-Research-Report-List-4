<p><p>Platform screen doors are safety systems used in transportation hubs to prevent accidents on railway platforms. There are three main types of platform screen doors: full-closed type, semi-closed type, and half-height type. </p><p>The full-closed type completely encloses the platform, providing maximum safety. The semi-closed type has a partial barrier, offering a balance between safety and accessibility. The half-height type only covers the lower half of the platform, prioritizing cost-effectiveness. Each type caters to specific needs and requirements of different transportation systems.</p></p>
