<p><p>The portable battery market consists of various types, each with unique characteristics. Lithium-Ion batteries offer high energy density and are widely used in smartphones and laptops. Lithium-Polymer batteries are lighter and more adaptable in shape, making them ideal for slim devices. Nickel-Cadmium batteries are robust but have lower energy density and are being phased out due to environmental concerns. Nickel-Metal Hydride batteries are more eco-friendly than nickel-cadmium, providing better capacity for hybrid vehicles and rechargeable consumer electronics.</p></p>
