<p><p>Airborne electro-optical pods are versatile devices used for capturing high-resolution images and videos from aerial platforms. These pods can be categorized into two types: multispectral and hyperspectral. Multispectral pods capture images in multiple wavelengths, allowing for analysis of various features such as vegetation health and soil composition. Hyperspectral pods capture images in hundreds of narrow and contiguous bands, enabling detailed analysis of materials and objects. Both types of pods are widely used in applications such as surveillance, reconnaissance, agriculture, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
