<p><ul><li>Oligomers</li><li>Monomers</li><li>Pigments/Non-reactive/Additives</li><li>Photo-initiators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Energy curing market consists of various types including oligomers, monomers, pigments/non-reactive/additives, and photo-initiators. Oligomers are pre-polymers that improve flexibility and hardness of coatings. Monomers are reactive molecules that undergo polymerization, contributing to the curing process. Pigments/non-reactive/additives enhance the appearance and performance of cured products. Photo-initiators are compounds that initiate the curing process when exposed to UV or visible light. These components together play a vital role in the energy curing process in industries such as automotive, electronics, and packaging.</p></p>
