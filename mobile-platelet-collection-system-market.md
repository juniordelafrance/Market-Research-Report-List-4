<p><ul><li>Centrifugation</li><li>Membrane Filtration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile platelet collection systems are used for the separation of platelets from whole blood for transfusion purposes. The two main types of mobile platelet collection systems are centrifugation and membrane filtration. Centrifugation utilizes high-speed spinning to separate platelets from other blood components, while membrane filtration uses a porous membrane to selectively filter out platelets. Both methods are efficient in collecting platelets and are commonly used in healthcare settings for platelet transfusions.</p></p>
