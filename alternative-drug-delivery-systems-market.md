<p><p>Alternative drug delivery systems refer to methods of administering medication through routes other than traditional oral or injection routes. Nasal delivery involves administering drugs through the nasal cavity, while transdermal delivery involves applying medication onto the skin for absorption. Transmucosal delivery involves absorbing drugs through mucous membranes, such as those found in the mouth or under the tongue. Other types of alternative drug delivery systems include inhalation, rectal, and vaginal routes. These systems offer advantages such as improved bioavailability, rapid onset of action, and reduced side effects.</p></p>
