<p><p>Fall protection equipment for working at height includes safety harnesses, safety lanyards, anchorage connectors, fall arresters, lifeline systems, and other specialized gear. Safety harnesses are worn by workers to prevent falls and distribute the force of impact in case of a fall. Safety lanyards connect the harness to a secure anchor point. Anchorage connectors are used to create safe tie-off points. Fall arresters stop a fall in progress. Lifeline systems provide continuous protection while working at height. Other equipment includes ladder safety systems, guardrail systems, and personal fall protection devices.</p></p>
