<p><p>The Intelligent Pump market consists of two main types: Centrifugal Pump and Positive Displacement Pump. Centrifugal pumps use a rotating impeller to create a flow of liquid, while Positive Displacement pumps move fluid by trapping a fixed amount and forcing it through a discharge pipe. Both types of pumps are equipped with intelligent features such as sensors and control systems that optimize performance, increase efficiency, and reduce energy consumption. These advanced technologies make intelligent pumps essential for various industries requiring precise and reliable fluid management.</p></p>
