<p><ul><li>Tilting</li><li>Rocking</li><li>Orbital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>3D laboratory shakers are used in various scientific applications for mixing, shaking, and blending substances. There are three main types of 3D laboratory shaker markets: tilting, rocking, and orbital. </p><p>Tilting shakers use a 3D tilting motion to mix samples, ideal for applications requiring gentle mixing. Rocking shakers have a see-saw motion that provides a smooth mixing action, making them suitable for cell culture and staining procedures. Orbital shakers move in a circular motion, offering a more vigorous mixing action for tasks like culturing microbes or solubilizing compounds.</p></p>
