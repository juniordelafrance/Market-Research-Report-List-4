<p><p>Civil Aircraft Ambulifts are specialized vehicles designed to transport passengers with reduced mobility on and off airplanes. There are two main types of ambulifts in the market - SideBull and FrontBull. SideBull ambulifts are equipped with a side-mounted lifting platform, allowing for easy access to the aircraft from the side. On the other hand, FrontBull ambulifts have a front-mounted lifting platform, providing direct access to the airplane's front door. Both types of ambulifts play a crucial role in ensuring safe and efficient boarding and disembarking for passengers with disabilities.</p></p>
