<p><p>Liberated mineral flotation reagent market types include flotation collectors, which enhance the attachment of minerals to air bubbles; flotation depressants, which prevent certain minerals from attaching to air bubbles; flotation activators, which increase the adsorption of collectors on mineral surfaces; and others like frothers and dispersants that aid in the separation process. Each type of reagent plays a crucial role in the flotation process, ultimately determining the efficiency and effectiveness of mineral separation.</p></p>
