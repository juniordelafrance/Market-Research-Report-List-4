<p><p>The ophthalmic surgery microscopes market can be categorized into three main types based on their mounting configurations: hanging bracket, desktop bracket, and wall-mounted bracket. Hanging bracket microscopes are suspended from the ceiling, allowing for flexible positioning and unobstructed floor space. Desktop bracket microscopes are compact and sit directly on a surgical table, providing stability and ease of use. Wall-mounted bracket microscopes are affixed to the wall, conserving space while offering adjustable viewing angles, essential for precise surgical procedures.</p></p>
