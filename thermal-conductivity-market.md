<p><strong>The Thermal Conductivity Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Wire Method</li><li>Light Flash Method</li><li>Heat Flow Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal conductivity market types include the Heat Wire Method, Light Flash Method, and Heat Flow Method. The Heat Wire Method involves measuring thermal conductivity by applying a constant heat flux to a material through a wire. The Light Flash Method uses a flash of light to heat the material and measure its thermal diffusivity. The Heat Flow Method measures thermal conductivity by applying a temperature difference across a material and measuring the resulting heat flow. These methods are commonly used in industries such as building materials, electronics, and food processing.</p></p>
