<p><p>Invasive blood glucose meters utilize different types of technology to measure glucose levels in the blood. GDH-FAD meters use glucose dehydrogenase and flavin adenine dinucleotide enzymes for accurate readings. GOD meters rely on glucose oxidase enzyme, while electrochemistry meters measure glucose levels through an electrochemical reaction. GDH meters use glucose dehydrogenase enzyme, while other meters employ various methods for glucose detection. Each type of meter has unique pros and cons, catering to different user preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
