<p><p>The piezoelectric buzzer market is segmented into two main types: active and passive piezo buzzers. Active piezo buzzers contain an internal oscillator, allowing them to produce sound with a simple DC voltage, making them easy to use in various applications. Conversely, passive piezo buzzers require an external signal or waveform to generate sound, providing flexibility in tuning frequencies but necessitating more complex circuitry. Both types are widely utilized in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial applications for alarms and notifications.</p></p>
