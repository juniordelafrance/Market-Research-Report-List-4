<p><strong>The Automotive Seat Belt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ALR Three Point Seatbelt</li><li>ELR Three Point Seatbelt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive seat belt market features two primary types: ALR (Automatic Locking Retractor) three-point seatbelts and ELR (Emergency Locking Retractor) three-point seatbelts. ALR seatbelts lock into place once the belt is pulled out, providing a secure fit for child safety seats. In contrast, ELR seatbelts automatically adjust to the occupant's body but lock during sudden stops or collisions, ensuring protection. Both systems are essential for enhancing passenger safety and are widely adopted in modern vehicle designs.</p></p>
