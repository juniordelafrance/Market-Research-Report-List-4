<p><p>AC Power Transducers play a vital role in converting electrical signals to power measurements. These transducers come in various types based on the number of phases they support. Single-phase transducers are designed for single-phase power systems, while dual-phase transducers are used for dual-phase power systems. Multi-phase transducers are capable of measuring power in systems with multiple phases. Each type of transducer caters to specific power system requirements, providing accurate and reliable power measurements essential for efficient operation and management of electrical systems.</p></p>
