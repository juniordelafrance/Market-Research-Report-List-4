<p><p>Solar Photovoltaic (PV) Cells and Modules Market Types include Passivated Emitter Rear Cell (PERC), Tunnel Oxide Passivated Contact (TOPCon), Heterojunction Technology (HJT), Heterojunction Interdigitated Back Contact (IBC), and Others. PERC technology improves solar cell efficiency by reducing recombination losses, while TOPCon involves a thin tunnel oxide layer for better passivation. HJT combines different materials for higher efficiency, and IBC design reduces shading and improves electrical conductivity. Other technologies include bifacial cells, thin-film solar cells, and concentrator photovoltaics. Each type offers unique advantages for solar energy generation.</p></p>
