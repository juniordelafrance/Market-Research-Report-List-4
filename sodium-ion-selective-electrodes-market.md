<p><p>Sodium Ion Selective Electrodes (SISE) are crucial for measuring sodium ion concentrations in various applications. Market types include liquid membrane and PVC membrane electrodes. Liquid membrane electrodes utilize a hydrophobic organic solvent to facilitate sodium ion selectivity, ensuring high sensitivity and faster response times. In contrast, PVC membrane electrodes incorporate polyvinyl chloride to create a solid-phase interface, offering stability and ease of fabrication. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including environmental monitoring, healthcare, and food safety, highlighting their essential roles in ion detection.</p></p>
