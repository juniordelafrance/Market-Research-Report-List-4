<p><p>Sintered Fly Ash Lightweight Aggregates Market includes two main types: Class F and Class C. Class F sintered fly ash is produced from burning anthracite or bituminous coal and has low calcium content, making it suitable for applications requiring a low-calcium aggregate. On the other hand, Class C sintered fly ash is produced from burning lignite or sub-bituminous coal and contains a higher calcium content, making it suitable for applications requiring a high-calcium aggregate. Both types offer lightweight and durable properties, making them ideal for construction and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
