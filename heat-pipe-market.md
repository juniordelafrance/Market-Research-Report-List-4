<p><p>Heat pipe market can be categorized into various types such as Constant Conductance, Vapor Chamber, Variable Conductance, Diode, Thermosyphon, and Others. Constant conductance heat pipes maintain a consistent heat transfer rate, while vapor chamber heat pipes are flat and offer high thermal conductivity. Variable conductance heat pipes adjust heat transfer based on temperature fluctuations. Diode heat pipes allow heat flow in one direction. Thermosyphon heat pipes use gravity for circulation. Other types include loop heat pipes and rotating heat pipes, each with unique features and applications.</p></p>
