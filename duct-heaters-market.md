<p><p>Duct heaters can be classified into three main market types based on their application: terminal air heaters, main air heaters, and process heaters. Terminal air heaters are used to heat the air in a specific area or room, while main air heaters are used to heat air in a large HVAC system for an entire building. Process heaters are designed for industrial applications where precise temperature control is required, making them suitable for heating air in manufacturing processes or production facilities.</p></p>
