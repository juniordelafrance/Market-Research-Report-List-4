<p><p>Vibrating conveyors can be categorized into two main types: natural frequency types and forced vibration types. Natural frequency types are designed to operate at the natural frequency of the system, resulting in efficient and smooth conveying of materials. Forced vibration types, on the other hand, use external forces to generate vibrations for conveying materials. Both types offer unique benefits and are used in various industries for transporting bulk materials efficiently and effectively.</p></p>
