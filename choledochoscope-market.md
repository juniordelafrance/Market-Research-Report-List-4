<p><p>A Choledochoscope is a medical device used to examine the common bile duct. There are two main types of Choledochoscopes on the market: Rigid Choledochoscopes and Flexible Choledochoscopes. </p><p>Rigid Choledochoscopes are straight and rigid, allowing for precise navigation through the duct. They provide high-quality imaging but are less flexible than their flexible counterparts.</p><p>Flexible Choledochoscopes, on the other hand, are more maneuverable and can navigate bends and curves in the duct more easily. They are preferred for complex procedures that require flexibility and adaptability.</p></p>
