<p><p>Refined Tungsten market types are categorized based on the mineral sources of tungsten, namely Wolframite, Scheelite, and Ferberite. Wolframite is a mineral composed of iron and manganese tungstates and is a major source of tungsten. Scheelite is a calcium tungstate mineral that is commonly found in ore bodies along with other minerals. Ferberite is an iron tungstate mineral that is another important source of tungsten. These market types represent different mineral compositions and locations where tungsten is extracted and refined.</p></p>
