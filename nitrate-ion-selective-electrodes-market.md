<p><strong>The Nitrate Ion Selective Electrodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Membrane</li><li>PVC Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nitrate ion selective electrodes are classified into two main types: liquid membrane and PVC membrane. Liquid membrane electrodes consist of a non-aqueous solvent containing a nitrate ionophore, while PVC membrane electrodes use a plasticized polyvinyl chloride matrix containing an ionophore. Liquid membrane electrodes offer high selectivity and sensitivity but require careful maintenance, while PVC membrane electrodes are more robust and easier to use. Both types are commonly used in environmental monitoring, agriculture, and pharmaceutical industries for measuring nitrate levels in various samples.</p></p>
