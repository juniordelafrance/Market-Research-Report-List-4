<p><p>A bunionectomy is a surgical procedure to correct a bunion, a bony bump that forms at the base of the big toe. There are different types of bunionectomy procedures including osteotomy, exostectomy, and arthrodesis. Osteotomy involves cutting and realigning the bone, exostectomy focuses on removing the bunion bump, and arthrodesis involves fusing the bones in the affected area. Each type of bunionectomy procedure is tailored to the specific needs of the patient and the severity of their bunion.</p></p>
