<p><p>Electronic Health Records (EHR) market types include Web-Based, Client Server-Based, and Software as a Service (SaaS). Web-Based EHR systems are accessed through a web browser and hosted on remote servers. Client Server-Based EHR systems store data on a central server and accessed by client computers. SaaS EHR systems are hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed through the cloud for a subscription fee. Each market type offers different benefits and customization options to healthcare providers based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
