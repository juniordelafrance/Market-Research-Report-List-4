<p><p>Fibrotic bronchoscopy market consists of two main types of instruments: rigid bronchoscopes and flexible bronchoscopes. Rigid bronchoscopes are rigid, tube-like instruments used for visualizing and accessing the airways during bronchoscopy procedures. They provide good visualization and allow for therapeutic interventions. Flexible bronchoscopes, on the other hand, are flexible, fiber-optic instruments that provide navigational flexibility and access to smaller and more distal airways. They are preferred for their maneuverability and ability to reach difficult-to-access areas. Both types play a crucial role in diagnosing and treating respiratory conditions.</p></p>
