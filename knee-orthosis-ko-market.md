<p><p>Knee Orthosis KO market includes two main types - Dynamic Orthotics and Static Orthotics. Dynamic Orthotics are designed to provide support and stabilization while allowing for controlled movement of the knee joint. They are typically used for conditions like ligament injuries and post-surgical rehabilitation. On the other hand, Static Orthotics are more rigid and provide maximum support and immobilization for conditions like osteoarthritis and severe ligament damage. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of individuals seeking knee support and protection.</p></p>
