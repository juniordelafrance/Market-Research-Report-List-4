<p><p>Transformer oil pumps are essential components used to circulate oil in transformers for cooling and insulation purposes. There are two main types of transformer oil pumps: centrifugal pumps and axial flow pumps. </p><p>Centrifugal pumps are commonly used in transformer applications due to their efficiency in moving large volumes of oil at high speeds. On the other hand, axial flow pumps are designed to move oil in a straight line, making them suitable for applications where a steady flow is required. Both types play a vital role in ensuring the optimal performance of transformers.</p></p>
