<p><p>The Calcium Carbonate Filler market consists of three main types: Heavy Calcium Carbonate, Light Calcium Carbonate, and Crystal Calcium Carbonate. Heavy Calcium Carbonate is primarily used in applications requiring high density and opacity, such as paints and plastics. Light Calcium Carbonate, produced through precipitation, offers a finer particle size, enhancing its use in the paper and rubber industries for improved brightness and texture. Crystal Calcium Carbonate, characterized by its crystalline structure, is often used in high-purity applications, including pharmaceuticals and food products.</p></p>
