<p><p>The windshield wiper market consists of two main types: wiper blades and wiper arms. Wiper blades are the rubber blades that come into contact with the windshield to wipe away rain, snow, and debris. Wiper arms are the mechanisms that hold and move the wiper blades across the windshield. Both wiper blades and wiper arms are essential components for ensuring visibility while driving in adverse weather conditions, making them important products in the automotive industry.</p></p>
