<p><p>The automotive carbon ceramic brakes market comprises two main types: single disc brakes and multiple disc brakes. Single disc brakes utilize a single, large carbon ceramic disc for effective braking power, commonly found in high-performance sports cars. In contrast, multiple disc brakes incorporate several smaller carbon ceramic discs, enhancing braking performance and heat dissipation, often used in luxury and high-end vehicles. Both types offer superior performance and durability, providing lightweight solutions with reduced brake fade under intense driving conditions.</p></p>
