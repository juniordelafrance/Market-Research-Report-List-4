<p><p>Insecticides market includes various types such as Pyrethroids, Organophosphorus, Carbamates, Organochlorine, and Botanicals. Pyrethroids are synthetic insecticides that mimic natural pyrethrins. Organophosphorus compounds affect the nervous system of insects. Carbamates inhibit enzymes in insect nervous systems. Organochlorine compounds have been widely used but are now mostly banned due to environmental concerns. Botanical insecticides are derived from plants and are considered more environmentally friendly. Each type has its own unique properties and applications in pest control.</p></p>
