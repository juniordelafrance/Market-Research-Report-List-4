<p><p>Covid-19 testing market is categorized into Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT), Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and Neutralization Assay. RDT provides quick results by detecting viral proteins or antigens in the patient's sample. ELISA tests detect antibodies produced by the immune system in response to the virus. Neutralization assays measure the ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus. Each type of test plays a crucial role in identifying and monitoring Covid-19 infections.</p></p>
