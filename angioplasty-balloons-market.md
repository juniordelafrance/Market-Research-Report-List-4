<p><p>Angioplasty balloons are used in the treatment of heart conditions by opening blocked or narrowed blood vessels. The types of angioplasty balloons include Normal Balloon Catheter, Drug-Eluting Balloon (DEB) Catheter, Cutting Balloon Catheter, and Scoring Balloon Catheter. Normal Balloon Catheters are the most commonly used and inflate to open up the blocked vessel. DEB Catheters release medication to prevent re-narrowing of the vessel. Cutting Balloon Catheters have small blades to cut the plaque, while Scoring Balloon Catheters have tiny grooves to improve the vessel expansion.</p></p>
