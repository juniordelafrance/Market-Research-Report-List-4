<p><p>General contracting involves a contractor overseeing an entire construction project, from start to finish, including hiring subcontractors and managing the project. This type of contracting is responsible for the overall building process and ensures the project is completed within budget and on time. On the other hand, subcontracting involves a contractor hiring specialized subcontractors to complete specific tasks within a project. Subcontractors work under the general contractor and are responsible for their portion of the project as outlined in their contract.</p></p>
