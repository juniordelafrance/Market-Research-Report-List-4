<p><ul><li>Scanning Acoustic Microscope (SAM)</li><li>Confocal Scanning Acoustic Microscope (CSAM)</li><li>C-Mode Scanning Acoustic Microscope</li><li>Scanning Laser Acoustic Microscope (SLAM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acoustic Microscope Market Types are varied and cater to different needs within the industry. Scanning Acoustic Microscope (SAM) is the most common type, used for analyzing material structure and defects. Confocal Scanning Acoustic Microscope (CSAM) provides higher resolution imaging by focusing on a specific depth within the sample. C-Mode Scanning Acoustic Microscope offers improved lateral resolution through a specialized imaging technique. Scanning Laser Acoustic Microscope (SLAM) uses laser technology for high-resolution imaging and deeper penetration capabilities. Each type serves specific purposes in material analysis and defect detection.</p></p>
