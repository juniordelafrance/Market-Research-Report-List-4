<p><p>The distance measurement sensor market comprises various technologies serving diverse applications. Inductive sensors rely on electromagnetic fields for non-contact measurements. Ultrasonic sensors emit sound waves, while laser diode sensors provide high-precision distance readings. Photoelectric sensors utilize light beams, draw wire sensors use retractable cables, and image sensors capture visual data for distance calculation. Each type offers unique benefits tailored to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
