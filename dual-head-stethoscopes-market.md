<p><p>Dual head stethoscopes come in three main types based on the size of the diaphragm diameter. Stethoscopes with a diaphragm diameter less than 40mm are ideal for pediatric patients or smaller areas of the body. Those with a diaphragm diameter of 40-50mm are commonly used for general adult examinations. Stethoscopes with a diaphragm diameter more than 50mm are designed for specialty applications or for patients with larger body sizes. Each type caters to specific medical needs and preferences.</p></p>
