<p><p>The mechanical palletizers market is typically segmented into low-level and high-level palletizers. Low-level palletizers load products onto pallets at a height close to the ground, making them ideal for operations with limited vertical space and easier access for operators. High-level palletizers, on the other hand, stack products at elevated heights, which enhances storage density and can streamline the loading and unloading process. Both types serve to improve efficiency and automation in warehousing and distribution operations.</p></p>
