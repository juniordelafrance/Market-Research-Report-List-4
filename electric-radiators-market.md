<p><p>The electric radiators market consists of various types catering to different needs. Fan heaters are compact and provide quick heating through a fan blowing over an electric heating element. Convection radiators distribute heat evenly through a combination of convection currents and radiation. Infrared radiators emit heat through electromagnetic waves, providing targeted heating. Oil-based electric radiators use a heating element to warm up oil, which then radiates heat throughout a room. Each type offers unique features and benefits, catering to different preferences and requirements.</p></p>
