<p><p>Micro Blood Flow Biosensors are devices used to measure blood flow in small vessels. Two common types of these sensors are Piezo-electric Sensor Wires and Pressure Microcatheters. Piezo-electric Sensor Wires use the piezoelectric effect to convert mechanical stress into electrical signals for blood flow measurement. Pressure Microcatheters are small catheters equipped with pressure sensors to measure blood flow in vessels. Both devices are essential tools in healthcare for monitoring blood flow in critical applications such as cardiovascular procedures and microsurgery.</p></p>
