<p><ul><li>AC Power Adapter</li><li>DC Power Adapter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laptop adapter market primarily includes AC power adapters and DC power adapters. AC power adapters convert alternating current from a wall outlet into the direct current required by the laptop, often featuring universal compatibility with varying voltage levels. In contrast, DC power adapters supply power directly from a battery or automotive source, commonly used for portable or emergency charging. Both types are essential for providing reliable power solutions, catering to different scenarios and user preferences.</p></p>
