<p><p>Polarographic dissolved oxygen probes are crucial instruments used in various applications to measure the concentration of dissolved oxygen in liquids. These probes function through an electrochemical method, wherein a polarographic sensor detects the diffusion of oxygen molecules across a membrane and generates a current proportional to the oxygen concentration.
