<p><p>The refractory pan mixer market offers two main types: dry powder mixing and wet mixing. Dry powder mixing involves blending dry materials without the addition of water, resulting in a homogenous mixture. Wet mixing, on the other hand, involves using water to create a slurry mixture. Both types cater to different requirements in the refractory industry, with dry powder mixing being more suitable for certain applications and wet mixing offering advantages in others. Manufacturers cater to both markets to meet a range of customer needs.</p></p>
