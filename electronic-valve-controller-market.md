<p><p>Electronic Valve Controller market is segmented into three main types based on the type of actuation system used: Pneumatic, Electric, and Hydraulic. Pneumatic valve controllers utilize compressed air to operate the valve mechanism, providing quick response and accuracy. Electric valve controllers use electrical signals to control valve movements, allowing for precise and automatic control. Hydraulic valve controllers use hydraulic fluid to actuate the valve, providing high force and power. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different industry requirements.</p></p>
