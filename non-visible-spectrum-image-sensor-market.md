<p><p>The non-visible spectrum image sensor market comprises two main types: linear image sensors and area image sensors. Linear image sensors capture images in a single row, processing light sequentially to generate images, making them suitable for applications like barcode scanning and document imaging. In contrast, area image sensors capture two-dimensional images simultaneously, making them ideal for applications such as medical imaging, surveillance, and industrial inspections. Both sensor types play crucial roles in enhancing capabilities in various non-visible spectrum applications.</p></p>
