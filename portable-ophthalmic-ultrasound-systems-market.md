The market types include A-Scan, which measures the length of the eye, B-Scan, which provides cross-sectional images of the eye, Combined Scan, which combines both A-Scan and B-Scan capabilities, Pachymeter, which measures corneal thickness, and Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM), which provides high-resolution images of the anterior segment of the eye. These systems are portable, making them convenient for use in various settings such as clinics or mobile eye care units.</p></p>
