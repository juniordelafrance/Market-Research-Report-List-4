<p><p>Surgery forceps are essential tools used in various types of surgical procedures. Electrosurgical forceps are specifically designed to deliver electrical energy for cutting or coagulating tissues during surgery. They have insulated tips to prevent unintended tissue damage. On the other hand, normal surgery forceps are basic instruments used for grasping, manipulating, or dissecting tissues during surgeries. Both types of forceps play a pivotal role in ensuring precision, control, and efficiency during surgical procedures.</p></p>
