<p><strong>The Orthopedic Navigation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CT-Free Navigation Systems</li><li>Image-Free Navigation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthopedic navigation systems are used in surgical procedures to assist surgeons in accurately placing implants. CT-free navigation systems use intraoperative tracking devices to provide real-time guidance without the need for preoperative CT scans. Image-free navigation systems rely on fluoroscopy or electromagnetic fields to track surgical instruments. Both types offer precise and efficient navigation during orthopedic procedures, reducing the risk of errors and improving patient outcomes. These advanced technologies are revolutionizing the field of orthopedic surgery.</p></p>
