<p><ul><li>Socket Weld Connections</li><li>Butt Weld Connections</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pressure seal gate valves are commonly available in two market types based on their connection methods: socket weld connections and butt weld connections. Socket weld connections involve attaching the valve to the pipeline by inserting the pipe into a recessed area on the valve body and welding it into place. Butt weld connections involve welding the valve directly to the pipeline. Both types offer secure and durable connections suitable for high-pressure applications in industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and power generation.</p></p>
